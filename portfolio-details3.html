Smart Glove

  • Category: Arduino and Bluetooth Module
  • Client: Electrothorn 2020 ( In Top 6 Teams)
  • Project date: 04 Jan, 2020
  • Project URL: Click here to see details

The Aim of the project:

It helps the deaf dumb and blind people to communicate via app .It is a glove that could help the deaf, dumb and disabled people to communicate with others just by touching the tips of your fingers!! (by using the pressure sensors). This would give a pre-programmed output i.e set of text (which is totally customizable) and MP3(Sound) on our smart glove app thus helping others understand what the patient or the user wants,convientently.
